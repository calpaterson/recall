Recall is a social bookmarking service, but there's more to it than that...

Recall is a bit different

Decentralised - Anyone can run a server and host all or part of what is being shared by the world
Liberal - No one controls the content of bookmarks. No one controls the types of bookmarks.
(Maybe) Private - Keep your bookmarks private, even from the people running the server!

But it's still being worked on, and hardly anything works yet!
